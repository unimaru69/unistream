Preamble

This privacy policy describes how Unimaru ("we") collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you use the UniStream application ("the app"). By using the app, you accept the practices described below.

1. Data collected

1.1 Data you provide

  • Email address: for creating and managing your UniStream account.
  • IPTV credentials (server URL, username, password): required to access content. This information is stored securely on your device (iOS/macOS Keychain, Android Keystore, secure storage on other platforms) and encrypted on our sync servers.
  • Profile name and avatar: optional, to personalize the multi-profile experience.
  • Parental control PIN: stored as a SHA-256 hash; the plain PIN never leaves your device.

1.2 Usage data

  • Favorites and collections: channels, movies, and series you add to your lists.
  • Watch history: dates, durations, and playback progress.
  • Preferences: theme, language, playback options.

1.3 Technical data

  • Anonymous crash reports via Sentry: in case of a crash, we collect the technical trace, app version, device model, and OS version. No personally identifiable information is included.
  • Authentication logs: IP address and timestamp, retained for 30 days for security reasons.

2. Use of data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Provide the UniStream service and its features.
  • Sync your favorites, collections, history, and progress across devices.
  • Manage your subscription and payments.
  • Improve app stability (crash reports).
  • Contact you in case of critical security or account issues.

We never sell your data to third parties. We do not perform targeted advertising.

3. Third-party providers

UniStream relies on the following services:

  • Supabase (Europe): authentication and user data synchronization. Supabase Policy
  • RevenueCat: subscription management. RevenueCat Policy
  • Sentry: anonymized crash reports. Sentry Policy
  • Apple, Google, Microsoft: subscription payment processing depending on the platform used.

4. IPTV content

UniStream does not provide, store, or distribute any audiovisual content. Video streams are transmitted directly between your device and your IPTV provider's server. We do not have access to the content you watch or your server-side viewing history.

5. Storage and security

  • Data is hosted in Europe (Supabase EU).
  • IPTV passwords are encrypted when stored.
  • Communications with our servers use HTTPS/TLS.
  • Database access is restricted and logged.

6. Your rights (GDPR)

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Access: obtain a copy of your data.
  • Rectification: correct inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: delete your account and all your data (directly in the app via Settings > Account > Delete my account).
  • Portability: retrieve your data in a structured format.
  • Objection: object to the processing of your data.

To exercise these rights, contact us at unistream@unimaru.fr.

7. Retention periods

  • Account data: as long as your account is active.
  • After account deletion: erasure within 30 days.
  • Sentry crash reports: 90 days maximum.
  • Security logs: 30 days.

8. Children

UniStream is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. Parents using parental controls can restrict access to certain categories.

9. Changes

This policy may be updated. Substantial changes will be notified by email and in the app at least 30 days before they take effect.
