Welcome

UniStream is an IPTV player compatible with the Xtream Codes protocol. Watch live television, movies, and series across all your devices.

Available platforms

  • iOS and iPadOS
  • Apple TV (tvOS)
  • macOS
  • Windows
  • Linux (AppImage and Flathub)
  • Android

Key features

  • Live TV with electronic program guide (EPG)
  • VOD catalog and series with resume playback
  • Replay / Catch-up
  • Favorites, history, and collections
  • Multiple profiles with PIN protection
  • Parental controls by category
  • Cloud sync across devices
  • 14-day free trial

Requirements

UniStream does not provide any content. You must have an active subscription with an IPTV provider compatible with Xtream Codes.
