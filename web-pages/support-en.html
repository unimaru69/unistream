Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up UniStream?

UniStream does not provide content. You need an active subscription with an IPTV provider compatible with Xtream Codes. On first launch, enter the server URL, username, and password provided by your operator.

A stream is not working, what should I do?

  • Verify that your IPTV subscription is active with your provider.
  • Check that your internet connection is stable.
  • Try another channel to isolate the issue.
  • Contact your IPTV provider if a specific stream is not working.

How do I manage my subscription?

UniStream subscriptions are managed by the platform where you subscribed:

  • iOS, iPadOS, macOS, tvOS: Settings > your Apple ID > Subscriptions
  • Android: Google Play > Payments and subscriptions
  • Windows: Microsoft Store > My account

Can I use UniStream on multiple devices?

Yes, your favorites, history, and playback progress are synced across all your devices via your UniStream account.

How do I enable parental controls?

Parental controls are available with the Premium subscription. Go to Settings > Parental Controls to set a PIN and block desired categories.

I forgot my parental PIN

You can disable parental controls by signing in to your UniStream account again. This will remove the PIN and reset blocked categories.

Account deletion

You can delete your account directly from the app: Settings > Account > Delete my account. This action is irreversible and removes all your personal data from our servers.
